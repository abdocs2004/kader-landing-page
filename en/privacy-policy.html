Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Kader has operated its website and learning services since 2025. Our goal is to provide a clear and safe experience for parents, students, and visitors.

We collect only the information needed to provide the service, such as name, contact details, child age, and subscription or booking details. We use this data to respond to enquiries, manage sessions, follow up on progress, and improve our services.

We do not sell personal data and we do not share it with third parties for marketing. We may share limited information with trusted service providers only as needed to run the website or communication channels, and only under obligations to protect and use the data for service purposes.

We take reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration, and we keep it only for as long as needed to deliver the service and meet legal obligations.

If you want to know what data we hold, or request correction or deletion, contact us at info@kadertech.live.
