Why Kader

A curriculum that grows up with your child

Kader is a specialized online academy that teaches programming to kids and teens aged 6 to 18. Our curriculum is split by age stage, and every stage is built around what kids that age can actually grasp and enjoy — guiding your child from their very first coding steps all the way to becoming a confident, independent programmer.

1

A path from 6 to 18

No generic one-size course — each age band has its own tools, pace and projects, so your child is always learning at the right level.

2

One instructor, every session

Your child keeps the same dedicated instructor throughout the course, who actually knows their pace, strengths and what motivates them.

3

A platform built for live teaching

Our interactive classroom connects student and instructor directly — screen sharing, live code, and real conversation, not a recorded video.

4

Scheduling that fits your week

Pick session times that work around school and family life, with the option to reschedule when things come up.

Courses

The right track for your child's age

Three stages, each designed around what kids that age are actually ready for.

  1. 1 What they'll learn
    6–7

    First Steps in Code

    What they'll learn

    • Getting comfortable with computer parts and basic usage
    • Logical thinking and problem-solving through play
    • First steps in programming with Scratch — games & animation style
    • One hands-on project to finish every single session

    Tools used

    Scratch
  2. 2 What they'll learn
    8–9

    Building With Blocks

    What they'll learn

    • Deeper computer literacy and digital tools
    • Programming with PictoBlox for more structured logic
    • Hands-on applied projects built session by session
    • More advanced concepts introduced through mBlock

    Tools used

    PictoBlox mBlock
  3. 3 What they'll learn
    10–18

    Real-World Programming

    What they'll learn

    • Python programming from the ground up
    • Web development — building real, working sites
    • UI/UX design and mobile app development
    • Game development and an introduction to Artificial Intelligence

    Tools used

    Python Web Dev UI/UX Mobile Apps Game Dev AI

What makes Kader different

Built so progress is never a guess

Live, not recorded

Every session is real-time, one-on-one — your child can ask questions and get answers on the spot.

Two-way weekly evaluation

Instructors rate each student's progress every week, and students rate their instructor too — accountability runs both ways.

Automated parent reports

After every session, you get an email summary of what your child covered and how they did. No need to ask.

A hands-on project every session

Kids learn by building. Every single class ends with something they made themselves.

A final project & presentation

Each course ends with a real project your child presents — a portfolio piece they're proud of.

Certificate on completion

A certificate that marks the milestone and the new skills your child can show off.
